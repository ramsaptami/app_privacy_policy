Delete Your Account

Casa by Tessellate

You can delete your Casa account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what data is deleted and how to request deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account information — your phone number, name, email, and profile photo
  • Properties — all property records you own (landlords)
  • Tenancy records — agreements, invitations, and tenancy history
  • Payment records — rent payments, payment proofs, and utility payments
  • Service requests — maintenance requests and attached photos
  • Documents — uploaded agreements, ID proofs, and receipts
  • Settings — notification preferences, currency, and other settings

Delete From Within the App

  1. Open Casa and go to the Settings tab
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

Request Deletion by Email

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access it, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: privacy@tessellate.co.in

Please include the phone number associated with your account. We will process your request and delete all data within 30 days.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you are a landlord, all property and tenancy records will also be deleted. You can always create a new account later, but your previous data cannot be recovered.
