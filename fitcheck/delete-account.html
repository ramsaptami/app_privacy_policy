Delete Your Account

Fit Check by Tessellate

You can delete your Fit Check account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what data is deleted and how to request deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Body profile — your height, body proportions, and size calibrations
  • Fit check history — all past fit check results
  • Account information — your email, display name, and authentication record

Data stored only on your device (if you never signed in) is removed when you uninstall the app.

Delete From Within the App

  1. Open Fit Check and go to the Account tab
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

Request Deletion by Email

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access it, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: privacy@tessellate.co.in

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and delete all data within 30 days.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You can always create a new account later, but your previous data cannot be recovered.
