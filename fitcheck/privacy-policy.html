Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026 | Version 2.0

Fit Check ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application developed by Bits of Tessellate that helps you predict whether clothing items will fit based on your body profile. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

a) Body Profile Data

When you set up your avatar, you provide:

  • Gender
  • Height (in cm)
  • Body proportions: shoulder width, bust size, waist definition, hip width, thigh type, torso length
  • Hair color (optional, for avatar display only)

This data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google, it is also synced to our cloud database (hosted on Supabase) so you can access it across devices.

b) Product URLs

When you paste a product URL or share one from another app, we send that URL to our backend server to scrape publicly available product information (name, price, image, description). We do not store the URLs you check beyond your local fit history.

c) Fit Check History

Results of your fit checks (product name, image URL, fit score, warnings) are stored locally on your device. If signed in, this history syncs to our cloud database.

d) Account Information

If you sign in via Google, we receive your Google account email address and profile name through Supabase Auth. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google services.

e) Automatically Collected Data

We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting, or advertising trackers. We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage analytics beyond what Expo and Google Play infrastructure may collect by default.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Body profile: Used solely to generate personalized fit predictions for clothing items.
  • Product URLs: Used to scrape product information for fit analysis. URLs are processed in real-time and not retained on our servers.
  • Fit history: Stored to let you review past fit checks.
  • Account info: Used to authenticate you and sync data across devices.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is shared only with the service providers listed below, solely to operate the app:

  • Supabase — Stores your account and synced data as our database and authentication provider. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (via Supabase Auth) — Authentication only. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Hosts our backend API that processes your requests. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI — AI service used server-side to enrich product data (categorize clothing, identify materials). Your body data is never sent to this service; only product descriptions are processed. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — AI service used server-side for product enrichment. Same restrictions as above. Privacy Policy

No personal data is sold to any party. We have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local data is stored using AsyncStorage on your device.
  • Cloud data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using Expo SecureStore.
  • All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data persists until you clear it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data persists until you delete your account.
  • Fit history is limited to your 50 most recent checks.

6. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Delete your body profile from within the app.
  • Clear your fit history from within the app.
  • Sign out to stop cloud syncing.
  • Delete your account from within the app (Account tab > Delete Account), or via our web deletion page. All cloud data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Additional Rights for EEA/UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Legal basis: We process your body profile data based on your explicit consent (provided when you create your avatar). We process account data based on contractual necessity (to provide the service). We process product URLs based on legitimate interest (to deliver fit predictions you request).
  • Right of access: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may correct inaccurate data via the app or by contacting us.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your avatar or account. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

Cross-border transfers: Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States (where Supabase, Vercel, and AI providers operate). These transfers are protected by the providers' standard contractual clauses and data processing agreements.

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@tessellate.co.in. We will respond within 30 days.

8. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know: You may request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to delete: You may request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to opt-out of sale: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

Categories of personal information collected:

  • Identifiers (email, name via Google Sign-In) — for authentication
  • Body measurements (height, proportions) — for fit prediction
  • Internet activity (product URLs checked) — for real-time fit analysis, not retained
