Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026 | Version 3.0

Alate ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application developed by Bits of Tessellate that helps you predict whether clothing items will fit based on your body profile. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

TL;DR: Your body profile stays on your device. Product URLs you paste are scraped in real time and discarded. We don't sell data, don't run ads, don't track you across apps.

1. Data We Collect

a) Body Profile Data (device-only)

When you build your profile, you provide height and six body proportions: shoulder, bust, waist, hip, thigh type, torso length. This data is stored exclusively on your device via AsyncStorage. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

b) Product URLs (ephemeral)

When you paste or share a product URL, we send that URL to our backend to scrape publicly available product information (name, price, image, description, material, sizes) from the source website. The URL is processed in real time and discarded. We do not persist URLs with your identity on our servers. The fit check result is stored locally on your device as part of your fit history.

c) Fit Check History (device-only)

Results of your fit checks (product name, image URL, fit score, warnings, recommended size, timestamps) are stored locally on your device. This data is not synchronised to any cloud service.

d) Device Identifier

On first launch, the app generates a random UUID stored locally and includes it as an X-Device-Id header on backend requests. This identifier is used solely for rate-limiting (to prevent abuse) and is not linked to your identity, name, email, or body profile. It is regenerated if you reinstall the app.

e) Google Account (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google on the Account screen, we receive your email address and profile name via Supabase Auth. Sign-in is optional. Your body profile, fit history, and calibration work without an account. Signing in is currently used only for future cross-device sync features — which we will disclose here before launching.

f) Crash Reports

We use Sentry and Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and performance traces. These include stack traces, device model, OS version, and a random session identifier. No body profile data, URLs, or account details are included in crash reports. Crash data helps us fix bugs and is retained for 90 days.

2. Age Requirement

Alate is intended for users aged 16 or older. On first launch, the app asks you to confirm your age. If you indicate you're under 16, the app will not collect body profile data from you. Users under 16 who circumvent the age gate by providing inaccurate information are not our intended audience; we will promptly delete any data we discover was collected from someone under 16.

3. How We Scrape Product Data

Alate fetches publicly accessible product pages to extract fit-relevant information. Our scraping practice:

  • We identify ourselves with the User-Agent Alate/1.0 (+https://alate.app) so site owners can identify and contact us.
  • We respect robots.txt — if an origin's robots.txt disallows automated access to a product path, we bail with a "please visit the store directly" message.
  • We respect explicit opt-outs — any brand can request removal via our opt-out form. Listed brands are skipped on subsequent fetches.
  • We do not bulk-crawl — we only fetch URLs explicitly submitted by a user.
  • We do not redistribute catalogues — scraped product data is used to generate a fit verdict for the user who submitted the URL; it is not aggregated, resold, or served as a browseable catalogue.
  • We point users back to the merchant — the fit card links directly to the source store via a "View on Store" button and a "Data from {brand}" attribution line.

4. Service Providers

We use the following service providers strictly to operate the app. None of them receive your body profile data.

  • Vercel — hosts the backend API that processes scrape and fit-check requests. Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — hosts the opt-out registry and (optional) authentication. Body profile is NOT synced to Supabase. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude) — used server-side to enrich scraped product descriptions when the source site lacks structured data. Only public product text is sent, never body profile. Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini — used server-side as a fallback enrichment model under the same restrictions. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In via Supabase Auth (optional) — authentication only, if you choose to sign in. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — crash reporting, PII-scrubbed. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — native crash reporting, PII-scrubbed. Privacy Policy

No personal data is sold to any party. We have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months.

5. Security

  • All network communication uses HTTPS / TLS.
  • Local data (body profile, fit history, age confirmation, device ID) is stored in AsyncStorage, which is sandboxed per-app by the OS.
  • Authentication tokens (if you sign in) are stored in Expo SecureStore, backed by iOS Keychain / Android KeyStore.
  • Our backend enforces rate limits and input validation. URLs are validated before fetching to prevent SSRF.

6. Data Retention

  • Local data persists until you clear it or uninstall the app.
  • Crash reports are retained for 90 days.
  • Rate-limit counters reset every minute and are not persisted.
  • Brand opt-out entries are retained indefinitely so the opt-out persists across deployments.
  • Product URLs are never retained.

7. Your Rights

At any time you can:

  • Delete your body profile from Account → Delete my body profile.
  • Clear your fit history from the History screen → Clear history.
  • Sign out (if signed in) from Account → Sign out.
  • Request full account deletion via our web deletion page. All server-side account data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us at the address below.

8. Brand Opt-Out

If you operate a storefront and prefer Alate not scrape your site, submit an opt-out request at this page. Your origin will be added to our blocklist within 24 hours. We honour the opt-out even if it conflicts with our technical ability to scrape; we do not require justification.

9. Additional Rights for EEA/UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Legal basis: We process body profile data based on your explicit consent (via the age gate and profile setup). We process product URLs based on legitimate interest (delivering the fit prediction you requested). We process crash data based on legitimate interest (app stability).
  • Right of access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection, withdrawal of consent — all exercisable by emailing privacy@tessellate.co.in. We respond within 30 days.
  • Cross-border transfers: Our backend runs on Vercel and Supabase infrastructure, which may route data through regions outside the EU/UK. Standard contractual clauses apply via our providers' DPAs.
  • Automated decision-making: Fit predictions are generated algorithmically but are not legally significant. You can always disregard the recommendation.

10. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident:

  • Right to delete: exercisable via the in-app deletion controls or by email.
  • Right to opt-out of sale/sharing: we do not sell or share personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not penalise you for exercising your rights.

Categories collected: Identifiers (email, name — only if you sign in via Google); Sensory/Geolocation — none; Internet activity (product URLs, ephemeral); random device identifier (rate-limiting only).
