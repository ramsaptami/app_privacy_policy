Brand Opt-Out Request

Alate by Bits of Tessellate

If you operate a storefront and prefer Alate not to scrape your site, let us know. We will add your origin to our blocklist and stop requesting product pages from it.

How It Works

  • Alate fetches public product pages only when a user explicitly pastes or shares the URL.
  • We identify ourselves with the User-Agent Alate/1.0 (+https://alate.app).
  • We respect robots.txt: if your file disallows our path, we bail automatically.
  • We do not redistribute scraped data. Product information is shown only to the user who requested it, alongside a "View on Store" link back to your storefront.
  • If you want to opt out anyway — no justification required — send us the request below and we'll honour it within 24 hours.

Request Removal

Email us with:

  • Your store's origin (e.g. yourstore.com)
  • A brief note if you'd like to tell us why (optional)
  • Your name and role (so we can confirm the request is from someone authorised)

Email: privacy@tessellate.co.in

What happens next: We add your origin to our blocklist within 24 hours. Any user who pastes a URL from your store will see a "This brand has asked not to be scraped — please visit their store directly" card instead of a fit analysis. Users can still reach you by tapping through to your storefront.

Partnership Alternative

If you'd rather integrate with Alate — sharing your size charts and product data through a consented partnership so your customers get accurate fit predictions — we'd love to talk. The same email works: privacy@tessellate.co.in.
